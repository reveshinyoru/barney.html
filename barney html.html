Group 6 (Barney) "Rama and Sita"

"Rama and Sita" made by barneys talks about the love story of two ancient Indians, Raya and Sita.


Their love story started when Sita got married to the prince of Adyodha, Raya.
Unfortunately, their love story was cruel. Both of them were seperated from each other, missing each other. The prince decided to conduct a quest to find his love.

He had asked a troop of monkeys to help with the rest of the rescue. Rama has completed his mission after overcoming a number of obstacles. The mission, which had been ongoing for years, was completed.

Sita was saved from harm. Sita rushed into the blazing fire and pleaded with the Gods to let her burn to ashes if she had been unfaithful. She leaped, but amazingly, she was still alive. The Lord had intended to send her back to her loved ones.
